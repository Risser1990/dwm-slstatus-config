# DWM Keybindings Cheat Sheet
`Mod+shift+Space` - dmenu

## Apps
`Mod+Space` – Rofi | `Mod+Return` – Terminal | `Mod+B` – Brave | `Mod+E` – Geany  
`Mod+F` – Thunar | `Mod+G` – Steam | `Mod+M` – Thunderbird | `Mod+O` – OnlyOffice  
`Mod+P` – CopyQ | `Mod+Print` – Flameshot (GUI)

## Core DWM
`Mod+Shift+B` – Toggle Bar | `Mod+X` – Kill Window | `Mod+V` – View Layout  
`Mod+Shift+Return` – Zoom (Promote to Master)

## Stack Navigation
`Mod+↑/↓` – Focus Stack | `Mod+Shift+↑/↓` – Move in Stack  

## Master Size
`Mod+←/→` – Shrink/Grow Master Area

## Layouts
`Mod+H` – Tiled | `Mod+J` – Floating | `Mod+K` – Monocle | `Mod+L` – Cycle Layouts  
`Mod+Tab` – Toggle Floating
`Mod+Ctrl+Tab` – +Master | `Mod+Ctrl+Shift+Tab` – -Master

## Monitors
`Mod+, / .` – Focus Monitor | `Mod+Shift+, / .` – Send to Monitor

## Gaps
`Mod+- / =` – Adjust Gaps | `Mod+Shift+=` – Reset Gaps

## Tags
`Mod+[1-9]` – View Tag | `Mod+Shift+[1-9]` – Move to Tag  
`Mod+0` – View All | `Mod+Shift+0` – Tag to All

## Power
`Mod+Shift+Esc` – Rofi Power Menu | `Mod+Ctrl+Shift+Esc` – Quit DWM

## Volume
`Mod+F12` – Vol + | `Mod+F11` – Vol - | `Mod+F10` – Mute
